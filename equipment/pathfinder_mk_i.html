Pathfinder Mk. I

A customized, high performance vehicle that's a hybrid of Jeep and Sports Car, discreetly uparmored like an Armored Car.

From the outside it appears to be the exotic (but not unprecedented in the excess of 1980's Wall Street) Lamborghini LM002, fully loaded, in a futuristic matte dark grey. Inside it features a plethora of high tech equipment. It features a an impressive communications suite with a 40-channel CB with SSB and PA speakers, GPS navigation, celluar car phone, police scanner, world band, weather, and traffic radios, satellite and television receiver and VHS, Betamax, Laserdisc and CD decks with color monitor, and UHF, VHF, and shortwave transceivers. The built in winch is complemented by an onboard welder/generator/air compressor, oxyacetylene torch, comprehensive first aid kit, tool kit, pioneer tools, secure storage, trail vise, rear work light, light bar and fog lights, emergency flashers, spotlights, and oxygen and fresh water tanks, to ensure versatility and utility. In addition to being discreetly armored, it has several other defensive systems.
