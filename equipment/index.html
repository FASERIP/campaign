Custom Equipment

High-tech gear by Julian Constantine for himself and his allies.

  • Dawnbringer's Costume: Armored costume made to withstand the use of superpowered abilities.
  • Guardian Blaster Mk. I: A multifunctional weapon and utility device.
  • Sentinel Battlesuit Mk. I: An armored, ability-enhancing protective battlesuit loaded with gadgets.
  • Pathfinder Mk. I: A highly customized vehicle based on the Lamborghini LM002.
  • Defender Staff Mk. I: A telescoping/collapsible staff/spear with built-in gadgets.
