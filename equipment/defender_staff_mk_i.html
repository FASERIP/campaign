Defender Staff Mk. I

A telescoping/collapsible alloy pole with with retractable blades the ends. May be used as blunt or edged weapons in either extended or collapsed positions, and thrown in any configuration. Has many build-in gadgets and utility functions. They may connect end-to-end with the blade retracted to form a longer pole. Nightsteel knows how to throw them so they arc or ricochet to return when thrown, whether they hit or miss. They also contain load-bearing tether points, and have built-in retractable spools of 100 feet of Excellent Material Strength ultrathin line, with an Excellent Strength brake, Excellent Strength winder, and quick-detach points at either end.
