Marvel Universe Manhattan, 1986

System
TSR Marvel Super Heroes RPG Advanced
Universe
Marvel Universe
Era
1986-1992
Start
1986-02-15

Set in the late Bronze Age/Early Iron Age of mid-1980's to early 1990's Marvel Comics, this campaign is mostly a slice-of-life about the ordinary lives of extraordinary people, along with some heroic action.

It follows the daily life and adventures of Julian Constantine, AKA Nightsteel, a wealthy inventor and businessman living a double life as a street-level high-tech hero, as well as those of his friends, allies, rivals, and enemies.

Dramatis Personae

  • Nightsteel, AKA Julian Hieronymus Constantine: A singularly brilliant, wealthy, talented young man who always wanted to be a hero, and founded Steel Sentinels, Incorporated with his fortune to better protect Manhattan. A player character and the primary protagonist. He also invents a lot of custom hero gear for himself and his allies.
  • LOVELACE, AKA Ada Grace Turing: An advanced artificial intelligence created by Julian who became fully self-aware, LOVELACE is ostensibly the AI interface to Steel Sentinels corporate computer systems. Only Julian knows the truth, and that Ada Turing is a projected Alter-Ego. Deuteragonist of the campaign, LOVELACE is both a PC and NPC controlled jointly by the Player and Gamemaster, but primarily a Gamemaster-controlled NPC.
  • The Dawnbringer: An angelic construct, this divine synthezoid offers both healing and a sword to those in need, bringing light to the darkness of Manhattan's deepest reaches at night. The campaign's tritagonist, the Dawnbringer is both a PC and NPC controlled jointly by the Player and Gamemaster.
  • Ashtaret: An ancient Demon from terrible realms beyond, just trying to live her best immortal life in the dual-natured glamour and shadows of 1980's Manhattan. As the tetertagonist and sometimes minor antagonist, Ashtaret is both a PC and NPC controlled jointly by the Player and Gamemaster, but primarily a Gamemaster-controlled NPC.
  • Steel Sentinels: the eponymous, famed sub-heroes who are the elite operators of Steel Sentinels, Incorporated, and other company personnel. They are allies and subordinates of Julian/Nightsteel and are NPCs controlled by the Gamemaster.
  • An assorted cast of classic mid-1980's-to-early-1990's Marvel Comics characters, played as their canon for the era by the gamemaster, alterations for relationships with the player characters and organizations aside.
  • As many spur-of-the-moment NPCs, both throwaway and recurring, as are required.

Setting

The campaign is set in Marvel Universe Manhattan in the mid-80's to early 90's, and surrounding areas. With the exception of the player characters and their organization and associated NPCs, everything else is canon for the primary comics line of the era.
