Locations

  • Greymount Manor: A Gilded-Age, Beaux-Arts mansion in West Village owned by Julian and used as his personal headquarters.
  • Steel Sentinels Headquarters: A secure, high-tech complex on the river in Hudson Yards near the port and transit station, where Steel Sentinels, Incorporated is based. It consists of a mid-rise office tower attached to a parking garage, warehouse, and factory, with a perimeter wall and dock and rail access.
