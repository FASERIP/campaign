Greymount Manor

A grand, stately, Beaux-Arts mansion in West Village, the palatial estate has a private courtyard with peristyle, ornate, gated porte-cochère and portico, and an attached carriage house that was converted to a vehicle garage in the 1920's with direct street access. Greymount Manor is owned by Julian Constantine.

The cost of the building is +1CS due to its location in Manhattan.

Staff

Greymount Manor employs several staff: a butler, a secretary, four housekeepers, two cooks, two groundskeepers, and two mechanics, all Typical salary. Additionally the estate retains a Good salary.
