Nightsteel

Julian Hieronymus Constantine is a singular genius inventor, scientist, and engineer, and a very, very part time grad student at an extremely prestigious institution. Due to a quirk of inheriting distant Asgardian and Olympian blood, both, he has significant but undeveloped potential in the mystical arts of which he is only vaguely aware, though he has studied the subject extensively from a theoretical perspective. For the most part, Julian just wants to live his best life as a young Manhattanite, indulging his passion for inventing while occasionally engaging in heroics, adventurism, and vigilantism in his guise as Nightsteel.

After a childhood idolizing superheroes, he dedicated his life to becoming one, devoted himself to the study of anything that could be useful in the hero field: science, engineering, psychology, police procedure, martial arts, gymnastics, and, after graduating from high school and college very early, even a short stint in the Army when he was old enough to enlist. Ultimately he discovered too all too late into a career of vigilante adventurism what a grim and arduous business being a hero was. With a vast fortune from licensing and commercializing patents, Julian awoke to the realization that wealth was also a superpower, and founded Steel Sentinels, Incorporated, to help protect his beloved Manhattan than an individual hero ever could.

Tall and strongly built, long limbed and broad-shouldered, with red hair and green eyes that contrast his olive skin, Julian's features reflect his Nordic-Scandinavian, Gaelic-Celtic, Greek, and Italian heritage. He is friendly, approachable, and no-nonsense direct, but has a strong sense of humor. A fifty-fifty ambivert, he can be a little stubborn, pushy, or withdrawn and brooding sometimes, while being the life of the party others. He tries hard not to micromanage and focus on the bigger picture now that he has the company to handle the details, and mostly succeeds. He is also a lover and patron of the arts, theater, music, and animals, even if he can be cynical about people from time to time.
