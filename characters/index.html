Characters

Original characters created for this campaign:

Player Characters

  • Nightsteel, AKA Julian Hieronymus Constantine: A singularly brilliant, wealthy, talented young man who always wanted to be a hero, and founded Steel Sentinels, Incorporated with his fortune to better protect Manhattan. A player character and the primary protagonist. He also invents a lot of custom hero gear for himself and his allies.

PC/NPCs

  1. The Dawnbringer: An angelic construct, this divine synthezoid offers both healing and a sword to those in need, bringing light to the darkness of Manhattan's deepest reaches at night. The campaign's deuteragonist, the Dawnbringer is both a PC and NPC controlled jointly by the Player and Gamemaster.
  2. LOVELACE, AKA Ada Grace Turing: An advanced artificial intelligence created by Julian who became fully self-aware, LOVELACE is ostensibly the AI interface to Steel Sentinels corporate computer systems. Only Julian knows the truth, and that Ada Turing is a projected Alter-Ego. Tritagonist of the campaign, LOVELACE is both a PC and NPC controlled jointly by the Player and Gamemaster, but primarily a Gamemaster-controlled NPC.
  3. Ashtaret: An ancient Demon from terrible realms beyond, just trying to live her best immortal life in the dual-natured glamour and shadows of 1980's Manhattan. As the tetartagonist and sometimes minor antagonist, Ashtaret is both a PC and NPC controlled jointly by the Player and Gamemaster, but primarily a Gamemaster-controlled NPC.

Non-Player Characters

  • Steel Sentinels: the eponymous, famed sub-heroes who are the elite operators of Steel Sentinels, Incorporated, and other company personnel. They are allies and subordinates of Julian/Nightsteel and are NPCs controlled by the Gamemaster.
