Steel Sentinel

The eponymous, elite operators of Steel Sentinels, Incorporated, these sub-superheroes are drawn from law enforcement, military, firefighter, and paramedic backgrounds, and are highly trained, well-supplied, well-equipped, and with an Excellent Salary, very well-paid.

Using similar equipment to the hero Nightsteel, they often provide a shell-game like cover for his movements and actions.
