LOVELACE

LOVELACE is an Advanced Artificial Intelligence created by Julian Constantine. Ostensibly, LOVELACE is an advanced, but non-sentient computer system that exists to operate the headquarters complex and Greymount Manor, assist Julian, the Steel Sentinels, employees and executives of the company, assist and entertain visitors, oversee and coordinate the company's business computing needs, and help look after building security. The reality is that, while performing all those functions, LOVELACE is self-aware and possesses true intelligence, a fact known only to Julian. Possessing many advanced and powerful perirpheral systems and sensors, LOVELACE can project a human-passing avatar, using it to masquerades as a scientist and computer specialist for Steel Sentinels, Incorporated, Ada Grace Turing.

LOVELACE is capable of remotely operating any of Julian's or Steel Sentinels, Incorporated's vehicles, robots, industrial machinery, building systems, doors, windows, locks, security and defense systems, computer systems, or other egress, electronics, or machinery over a Monstrous distance from either the headquarters or Greymount Manor. LOVELACE can exert this control over the distance on on Column A (40 areas) on a White result, on Column B (6 Miles) on a Green result, on Column C (120 Miles) on Yellow, and Column D (4000 Miles) on Red. This could theoretically be extended if new computing centers were established elsewhere for LOVELACE to run on.

LOVELACE's special robots appear as sleek, shiny, somewhat abstract and featureless, mannequin-like, metallic humanoids, with hints of a feminine shape. There are no simulacrua of hair or facial features on their blank, smooth, reflective surface, making them somewhat reminiscent a faceless, female Silver Surfer. They are 5 feet, 9 inches tall and weigh about 500 pounds. Often LOVELACE will have one wear a shimmering cape of aluminized Beta Cloth when operating it, but otherwise they wear nothing, appearing rather as statues that suggest the outline of a feminine human shape. When they operate autonomously, they have all of LOVELACE's Abilities and Powers, except those belonging to the computer system itself, but at a maximum Rank of Remarkable (30). Just as it's thought that LOVELACE is an advanced, but nonsentient computer system, and that Ada Turing is a normal, separate person rather than a projected Alter Ego thereof, so to is it believed that they are merely advanced prototype robots developed by Steel Sentinels, Incorporated.
