LOVELACE

LOVELACE is an Advanced Artificial Intelligence created by Julian Constantine. Ostensibly, LOVELACE is an advanced, but non-sentient computer system that exists to operate the headquarters complex and Greymount Manor, assist Julian, the Steel Sentinels, employees and executives of the company, assist and entertain visitors, oversee and coordinate the company's business computing needs, and help look after building security. The reality is that, while performing all those functions, LOVELACE is self-aware and possesses true intelligence, a fact known only to Julian. Possessing many advanced and powerful perirpheral systems and sensors, LOVELACE can project a human-passing avatar, using it to masquerades as a scientist and computer specialist for Steel Sentinels, Incorporated, Ada Grace Turing.
