Steel Sentinels, Incorporated

Based out of their corporate headquarters in Hudson Yards, Manhattan, all services provided by Steel Sentinels Incorporated are pro bono, and they never charge for services rendered, even at their private hospital. Since their inception, the company's agents, uniformed and otherwise, have become ubiquitous in their area of operations of downtown and midtown Manhattan. Though their uniformed security, construction, and maintenance personnel are the most visible, the eponymous Steel Sentinels are by far the most famous, even if few ever actually see them in action. Private funding makes them answerable only to the law of the land, although they do nonetheless strive for transparency and community stakeholder engagement.
